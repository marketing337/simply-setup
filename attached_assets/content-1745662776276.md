Who can use a virtual office?

Any company, startup, freelancer, or brand can make use of a virtual office. It serves as a valuable resource for business professionals seeking to launch a new venture or extend their presence across India. When compared to traditional rental options, it's a more cost-effective choice.

Where does Aaddress help businesses open virtual offices?

Aaddress helps both-small and large businesses set up their virtual presence in various locations all across India. Some of the prime locations are Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Gurugram and Kolkata.

Do I receive a dedicated phone number?

A dedicated phone number is not included as part of the Virtual Office service, but you can obtain one separately through IVR service providers.

Will my clients/customers figure out that I'm using a virtual office?

The concept of a 'virtual office' is to provide an alternative to renting or owning an office space. It acts like a new business solution. But in reality, our virtual offices are like working business centres and coworking spaces in India. You can access meeting rooms, day passes, reception services, and other office benefits.

Why should I choose a virtual office at Aaddress?

There are numerous reasons to choose Aaddress.in for setting up your virtual office. Some of the key benefits include not only providing you with a premium business mailing address but also the necessary GST-compliant documents for your GST and business registrations. Additionally, you can enjoy the advantages of physical office facilities, including access to meeting rooms, day passes, participation in entrepreneurship events, reception services, unlimited tea and coffee, prominent display of your company's name, and much more.

What all documents will be provided under Virtual Office for GST Registration?

List of documents provided: Proper address proofs in the desired format as per the guidelines outlined by the GST authorities, service agreement, and a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the legal occupant of the premises along with supporting utility bills.

Can a GST number be applied on a virtual office address?

Yes, with proper documentation, GST number can be obtained with Virtual Office. Address Proof, Agreement and NOC from legal owner is provided.

Does Google accept virtual address?

Yes, Google does accept a virtual address to create a GMB profile and business verification code, but only if your business is a service-area business. A virtual address can be used for business correspondence.

Can I use virtual address for Amazon business?

Yes, you can use a virtual address for your Amazon business to set up an account. You need to ensure that virtual office provider is reputable providing all necessary documents.

Do banks accept virtual addresses? Can I open a bank account with a virtual address in India?

Yes, banks accept virtual addresses for various purposes, including opening a bank account. Yes, you can open a bank account with a virtual address in India. Many banks in India accept virtual addresses for account opening, for individuals and businesses.
